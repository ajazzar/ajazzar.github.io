I created a page that allows the user to play a game with the computer. In this game, the player rolls two dice and the computer rolls two dice. The Math.random() method is used to generate each roll of a die, from 1 to 6. The sum of the computer’s roll is compared to the sum of the player’s roll and whoever has the greater sum is the winner of that round.


I created an html sign-in page for a website. The page obtains the following information: the user’s first and last names, street address, city, state, zip code, email address, and phone number. I also added a username and password section. The username has to be between 4 and 20 characters. The password has to be between 4 and 12 characters, contain at least one digit, and contain at least one uppercase and one lowercase character.


I created a page that can be used to add children of various age groups to one of three Soccer Leagues: Junior, Intermediate, and Senior. The Junior League is for children ages 4–7, the Intermediate League is for children ages 8–11, and the Senior League is for children ages 12–15.


I created a button on a web page that can be clicked to starts a countdown. After the countdown is completed, the word "blastoff!" is shown.
