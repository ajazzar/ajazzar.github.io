I made a Landscape website in my PHP class. I created a PHP page that extracts a customer’s bill amount and the amount paid. Then it calculates the amount due. If the amount due is greater than 0, an email is generated and sent to the customer with that amount in the message. If the amount due is zero or there is a credit, a thank-you email is generated that thanks the customer for his or her payment.


I made a fictional Doctor Who website. I created a database named doctorWho. Then I created a page that allows Doctor Who’s assistant to add a new patient record. Then, I made a page that displays three or more fields from each of the patient records added. The patient’s first and last names and a unique identifier along with other information are displayed on that page. I created a page that display all records from any one table from the Doctor Who database. I named this page getRecords.php.
